Hi! Thanks for visiting. Feel free to click on these items.
It takes minitues to load the data (sorry because the server is located in China).
Or you can 'wget' the website to your local machine and open it.
For example, type this in your terminal: wget http://39.107.87.238/arctic_tern_2019.html

If you still have trouble loading, try Apus apus or Falco amurensis data first, because they are relatively small.
To spead up the motion, pull the "fps" bar to 10. To replay the motion, click on the "replay" botton. To limite the time interval, you can drag the triangles on the on the time bar.

If you find that some of the pictures are strange and make no sense, that's normal, because we simply use web crawler to request pictures from bing.com.
They didn't go through human review, therefore, just for fun! :)
